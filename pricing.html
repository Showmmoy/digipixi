Got Questions?

Frequently Asked
Questions.

How long does a website take? +
Most websites are delivered within 2–3 weeks. For urgent projects we offer a 2–3 day fast-track delivery. Timeline depends on how quickly you can provide content and feedback.
Do you require a deposit? +
Yes — we require a 50% deposit before starting work. The remaining 50% is due upon completion before we hand over the final files or launch the website.
What do I need to provide? +
We'll need your business information, any existing logos or brand materials, content (text and images) for your website, and feedback during the design process. Don't worry — we guide you through everything.
Do you offer payment plans? +
Yes! For larger projects we can arrange installment payments. Contact us to discuss a payment plan that works for your budget.
Will my website work on mobile? +
Absolutely. Every website we build is fully mobile responsive and tested across all devices and browsers before delivery.
Do you provide hosting? +
We can recommend hosting solutions and help you set everything up. Hosting costs are separate from our design fees and depend on your chosen provider.
What happens after launch? +
All packages include free post-launch support. We also offer ongoing maintenance packages starting from £59/month to keep your website updated and secure.
Can I update the website myself? +
Yes! We build on WordPress with Elementor so you can easily update content yourself. We also provide a basic training session so you feel confident managing your site.
